THE SCIENCE

FORMS OF ENERGY, CONNECTIVITY, AND PROTECTION IN THE IPHONE.



Lithium Battery

Converting chemical energy to electrical.
Lithium batteries are light-weight, energy dense batteries that make them ideal for portable devices with a longer battery life.

Accelerometer Sensors

Detercting motion using acceleration. By detecting motion on three different axies, allowing a digial representation of a direction and magnitude of any motion.

Proximity Sensors

Sensors that can detect input without touch. By using proximity sensors, the iPhone is able to be controled without any touch. This allows users to pickup calls by just putting the phone close to their face.

GPS Navigation

Using satelites to position the iPhone. By connecting to satelities in Earth's orbit, the iPhone is able to position itself on Earth's surface. This allows the iPhone to naivgate it's users across the world.

Ceramic Shield

Growing nanocrystles for stronger glass. In order to give the iPhone it's remarcably strong screen, ceramnic nanocrystles were grown in the glass for extra durability.

SINUSUDIAL MOTION IN THE IPHONE.

When using any scrollbar in the iPhone, sinusudial motion is applied. At the start of the scroll, the speed of the scrollbar is at it's absolute lowest, but it slowly starts rising. Once at the apex of force, and where you would let go, the scrollbar is at it's highest point of speed. After the release, due to the momentum the scrollbar continues going, but slowly slows down, until reaching equlibrium at rest once more.

Don't believe us? Try it out on this website! This monitor is a touchscreen!


The motion of this scrollbar can be perfectly represented by speed-over-time sinusudial graph. An example is provided on the right of this paragraph.

Our improvments

WAYS TO IMPROVE THE IPHONE.

Solid-state Batteries
A Solid-state Battery has absolutely no liquid in it, compared to a lithium-ion battery. This means that a Solid-state Battery is able to have an increased battery density, at a lower price, and at a lower risk of damage. Solid-state Batteries are currently in development to be commericalized to be put in our devices, but working models have existed since at least 2013.
Saphire Shield Glass
To make the screen more durable on the iPhone, instead of growing ceramic nanocrystles, saphire nanocrystals should be grown instead. Saphire is much more scratch and shatter resistant than ceramnic, and would make the phone much more durable. This technology is currently being used in several watches, so upscaling production would be the only issue.
Higher-End Cameras
Instead of using a puny 2-megapixel sensor, the future iterations of the iPhone should instead be using 32, 64, or even 128-megapixel sensors to capture more quality. Future cameras should also have better low-light capabilities to ensure excelent photos in any lighting. Better camera sensors and lens are already being made, putting them in the next iPhone won't be a problem at all.
